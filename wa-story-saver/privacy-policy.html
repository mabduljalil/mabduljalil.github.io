We do not collect, store, or share any personal information from users of the App. The App does not require you to create an account, log in, or provide any personal data to function.

The App reads WhatsApp status files (images/videos) that you have explicitly selected via the Android Storage Access Framework (SAF). These files are accessed locally on your device and are not transmitted, uploaded, or shared with us or any third party.
